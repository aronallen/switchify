Switch

Below is my switch project, its based on jQuery and very simple to use/extend.

Most of the CSS is calculated within the view, the only thing you need to do is provede a size attribute (that is the height) when initiating the switch, the width, font-size, etc. will be calculated automaticly, while considering strings lengths...

There is also a shading attribute, if this is set to true, there will be a lot of nice gardients and shadows on the switch.

Some bug in in Safari (5.1.1) and Chrome (15.0.8...) causes the pink area to bleed the rounded corners, even though overflow:hidden is on the containing DIV, Firefox (7.0.1) renders the content as expected.
